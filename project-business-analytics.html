PROJECT OVERVIEW

A data-driven approach to predicting and improving customer satisfaction in the restaurant industry through machine learning techniques. This project analyzes factors affecting customer satisfaction and provides actionable recommendations for business improvement.

The Business Analytics DW project combines data warehouse architecture with advanced analytics to extract meaningful insights from restaurant customer data. By leveraging statistical methods and machine learning algorithms, the project identifies key factors that influence customer satisfaction and provides a framework for data-driven decision making.

SKILLS

Data Analysis

Exploratory data analysis with statistical methods and visualization techniques to uncover patterns and relationships in customer data.

Machine Learning

Implementation of multiple machine learning models including Random Forest, Logistic Regression, and Naive Bayes to predict customer satisfaction.

Feature Analysis

Feature importance analysis to identify key satisfaction drivers and prioritize business improvements.

Model Evaluation

Comprehensive model evaluation using ROC curves, AUC metrics, and confusion matrices to ensure reliable predictions.

KEY FINDINGS

  • Service quality and food ratings are the strongest predictors of customer satisfaction
  • Loyalty program members show significantly higher satisfaction rates
  • Wait time has a strong negative correlation with customer satisfaction
  • Customers dining for celebrations report higher satisfaction levels

PROJECT CONCLUSION

The Business Analytics DW project demonstrates the power of data-driven decision making in the restaurant industry. By identifying key factors that influence customer satisfaction, businesses can prioritize improvements that will have the greatest impact on their bottom line.

The machine learning models developed in this project provide a reliable framework for predicting customer satisfaction based on various service and experience factors. This predictive capability allows businesses to proactively address potential issues before they impact customer satisfaction.
